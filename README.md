OSINT (_Open-Source Intelligence_) adalah sebuah metode pengumpulan, evaluasi, dan analisis informasi dari sumber-sumber yang tersedia secara publik untuk tujuan investigasi atau intelijen. Dalam dunia _cybersecurity_, OSINT adalah salah satu kemampuan paling dasar dan krusial. Seorang analis keamanan, _ethical hacker_, maupun investigator menggunakannya untuk memetakan jejak digital (digital footprint) suatu target—baik itu individu maupun organisasi—tanpa perlu melakukan peretasan sistem sama sekali. Sumber informasi ini sangat luas, bisa berasal dari media sosial, jejak mesin pencari, forum publik, repositori kode seperti GitHub, hingga berita dan catatan publik lainnya.

Melalui tantangan ini, kamu akan diajak untuk mempraktikkan langsung pola pikir (mindset) seorang investigator OSINT. Kamu tidak dituntut untuk mencari celah keamanan kode atau membobol sistem, melainkan merangkai kepingan-kepingan informasi publik yang berserakan di internet menjadi satu petunjuk yang utuh dan logis. Kemampuan untuk mencari, memilah, dan menghubungkan data-data yang awalnya tampak sepele inilah yangmenjadi senjata utama dalam memecahkan berbagai kasus.

## The Man Who's Been Missing

_An investigation into the digital footprint of a ghost._

### Status Report

An emergency investigation has been initiated. The subject, known as **'Mas Anas'**, has completely vanished. His partner has reported that he has not responded to any communications for over 24 hours, and every attempt to establish contact has been met with absolute silence. You are now tasked with stepping in to track him down.

From previous intelligence gathering, his Instagram account (@cnstllx) was successfully uncovered. The digital traces left there suggest he has gone off somewhere, but the trail goes cold before revealing his destination. There is one crucial piece of context, however: lately, he had been talking constantly about his high school days, expressing a deep nostalgia and a strong desire to reunite with his old OSIS (Student Council) friends.

It is highly likely his disappearance is directly tied to his past. Your objective is to pick up where the previous investigation left off, search through his fragmented digital footprint, and uncover the details of his history to help locate him.

### Data Requirements

To finalize your investigation report and point the search in the right direction, you must extract the following credentials from the subject's history:

- **Real Name:** The subject's full, actual name.
    
- **Cabinet Identifier:** The exact name of his OSIS cabinet.
    
- **GitHub Handle:** The username of its github account.
    

### Submission Format

Submit your findings as a flag in the exact format below:

> **GDGOC{fullname_cabinet_github}**

**Example:** `GDGOC{cristiano-ronaldo_grup-keluarga_ronald00}`

_Note: All input must be strictly in lowercase. Do not include spaces (use `-` instead)._
